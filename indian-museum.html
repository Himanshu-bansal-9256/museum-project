Indian Museum, Kolkata

About

The Indian Museum (formerly called Imperial Museum of Calcutta ) before independence, is a massive museum in Central Kolkata, West Bengal, India. It is the ninth oldest museum in the world and the oldest and largest museum in Asia, by size of collection.
